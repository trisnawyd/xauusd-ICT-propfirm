### 1. What It Is
Nonfarm Payrolls measures the net change in U.S. jobs each month, excluding farm, household, and non-profit workers. It's the Fed's primary labor-market pulse and a top-tier driver of rate expectations — a surprise in either direction can reprice rate-cut odds within minutes.

### 2. Market Impact (vs 95K forecast)
- **Stocks:** Beat → rally (growth strength); miss → selloff on recession fears, esp. rate-sensitive tech.
- **Bonds:** Beat → yields up / prices down (rate-cut bets fade); miss → yields down / prices up (rate-cut hopes + flight to safety).
- **USD:** Beat → DXY strength; miss → USD weakness.
- **Gold (XAU/USD):** Inverse to USD — **weak print is bullish gold**, strong print pressures gold via higher real yields + stronger dollar.

A sub-100K print confirms the cooling trend and would mark a notable inflection. **Watch for downward revisions to prior months** — a recurring pattern that often moves price more than the headline.
