**1. What It Is**  
Nonfarm Payrolls measures the net change in U.S. jobs outside farming each month. It's a key gauge of economic health and labor market strength. Investors watch it closely because it heavily influences Fed rate decisions and overall growth outlook.

**2. Market Impact**  
- **Stocks**: Likely modest upside if near forecast; weaker reading could spark selloff on recession fears.  
- **Bonds**: Stronger-than-expected jobs data pushes yields higher (prices lower) as rate cut odds fall.  
- **Currency**: USD strengthens on hot data, weakens on soft print.  
- **Other**: Gold/oil may dip on strong USD; risk assets volatile.

**3. Historical Context**  
Job gains have slowed from stronger prior levels, with April at +115K. The trend shows cooling but still positive additions, signaling a softening labor market without sharp collapse.
