# News Analysis — Gold Sell-Off Drivers (2026-06-23 01:56 UTC)

## Driver 1 — Hawkish Fed SEP shift (primary, structural)
- Updated Fed Summary of Economic Projections shows a **major hawkish shift: nine officials now forecasting at least one rate HIKE this year**.
- Higher-rate expectations are structurally bearish for non-yielding gold. This is likely the heavier weight and is **not on the MT5 econ calendar** (which only shows a 2-Year Note Auction 20:00 UTC MEDIUM).

- Monday's 843-pip rip priced maximum Hormuz-closure fear. That premium is bleeding out as the market treats the **US–Iran MOU (signed Jun 17 at Versailles; ceasefire extended 60 days; Hormuz reopening underway since Jun 18)** as the base case.
