## Gold-Specific Read — WHY gold is FALLING into a war escalation
Counter-intuitive but clean once you see the drivers:
1. **War premium already priced** — gold has carried a geopolitical bid for months; this flare-up adds little new.
2. **Response judged measured/contained** — anti-ship + air-defense targets, not nuclear sites or regime-change / ground war. Brent's muted +3.3% confirms the market's "limited" read.
3. **Rising real yields** — 10Y at 4.56% (1-mo high) directly offsets the haven bid.
