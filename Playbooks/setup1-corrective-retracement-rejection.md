# Setup 1: Corrective Retracement Rejection (HTF SHORT)

**Pattern:** Price makes a corrective bounce in a D1 downtrend, hits unmitigated OB or FVG in premium zone, then rejects.

---

## Entry Criteria

- D1 confirms downtrend (BoS down visible)
- H4/H1 bounce reaching premium zone (61.8%+ retracement or unmitigated OB)
- M5 shows rejection candle at OB/FVG (bearish engulf or pinbar)
- M1 confirms lower high printed

## Targets

Previous swing low or next unmitigated SSL

## Stop Loss

Above the OB/FVG that rejected

## Minimum R:R

1:2
