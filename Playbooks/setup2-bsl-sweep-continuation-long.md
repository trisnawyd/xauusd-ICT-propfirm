# Setup 2: BSL Sweep + Continuation Long (HTF Corrective Recovery)

**Pattern:** Price sweeps buy-side liquidity (pokes above a swing high), returns to OB/FVG, then resumes recovery uptrend.

---

## Entry Criteria

- HTF recovery structure intact (H4/H1 uptrend confirmed)
- M5 or M15 BSL sweep visible (wick above prior high)
- M5 returns to OB or FVG zone and holds
- M1 prints higher low after sweep low

## Targets

Previous swing high or next HTF resistance

## Stop Loss

Below BSL sweep low

## Minimum R:R

1:2
